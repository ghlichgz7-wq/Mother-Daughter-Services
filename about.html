Our Story

Gloria Bond started the service in 2013, after having seen a lady waiting with her shopping for a lift home. Gloria and her husband stopped and gave the lady a lift home and helped take her shopping in for her, of which the lady was very grateful. It was here the idea was birthed, to begin a service where people 'knew' who was coming to pick them up, and in having a team of people that would become not just a transport provider, but a friend to the people of the community.

Gloria's heart for the service was (and still is) that the ethos of Mother-Daughter Services be:

  • Valuing people
  • Enabling and Supporting them in what they need
  • Honouring them for who they are

The name 'Mother-Daughter Services' was thought of, so that we could be like a mother to the younger people or a daughter to the older people.

The 'Mother-Daughter Services' team are chosen for their heart for people of the community. The team love meeting people and supporting them through enabling them to get to places, whether it be transport to appointments, shopping, or to outings, and also keeping clients gardens tidy. The team are happy to accompany their clients in appointments, liaise with families where required, go with them in their shopping experience, or shop 'for them'. Sometimes people just like to go for a drive to have a change of scenery and have a refreshing break away from their normal surrounds.

Our team also do gardening, some people feel motivated in seeing us in their garden, and like to join with us in gardening, others can just enjoy having their garden done for them.

People who engage Mother-Daughter Services not only meet a companion driver or gardener, but in fact meet 'new friends'.
